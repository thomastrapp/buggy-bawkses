Detailed Description

Manages creation and deletion of Bars.

The Bartender is responsible for creating new bars and deleting old bars.

As the view (=camera) moves upwards new bars need to be added. The Bartender makes sure there's at least bar-pre-fill pixels full of bars on top of the view.

If a Bar becomes invisible (=it is not shown on the screen) it is deleted.
