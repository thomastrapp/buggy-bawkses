Detailed Description

Acts as a Wrapper for b2World and creates all entities.

A call to World::render or World::update is proxied to all child entities.

The World's coordinate space starts at 0 (bottom of the World) and grows upwards into negativity.

The reader may consider this drawing as a substitute for this bad explanation:
